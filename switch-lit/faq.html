What is Switch-Lit?

Switch-Lit is a creative writing platform designed for turn-based collaborative fiction, poetry, and prose. Each work becomes an imaginative conversation between any two creative writers: one starts, the other responds in kind, and the narrative world comes alive via alternating chapters. The final results are multi-perspective stories and genre-bending experiments that can read like collective dreams.

Launched publicly in 2024, Switch-Lit has been recognized as a 2025 SXSW Innovation Award Finalist ("Connection and Communication" category), a featured panelist at Harvard University's inaugural "Social Media Solutions for the Public Interest" workshop, and the Brooklyn Public Library's Community Incubator Grant program.

Switch-Lit is for anyone who loves storytelling and wants to explore the creative possibilities of collaborative writing. Whether you're an experienced writer or just starting out, if you're curious about co-creating narratives with another person, this platform is for you.

To start a story, click on "Start a Story" and fill out the story settings form. You can invite a writing partner by email, set word and time limits, and decide who writes first. Once your partner accepts, you can begin your collaborative journey!

We limit active stories to ensure quality engagement and prevent burnout. This encourages writers to focus on their current collaborations and complete them before starting new ones.

We welcome all genres and styles of collaborative fiction, poetry, and prose. Stories should be completed through the Switch-Lit platform with alternating chapters between two writers. There are no strict formatting requirements beyond what the platform provides.

Once your collaborative story is complete, both writers must agree to submit it for publication consideration. Navigate to your completed story and click "Submit for Publishing." Our editorial team will review it within 4-6 weeks.

At this time, we only accept stories that were created through the Switch-Lit platform to maintain the integrity of our collaborative process. However, we're always interested in hearing about innovative collaborative projects, so feel free to contact us.

Our editorial process involves light copyediting for clarity and consistency while preserving the unique voice of each collaborative piece. We work closely with both writers throughout the process. Published stories are featured in our Library and may be promoted through our social channels.

Copyright is jointly held by both collaborating writers. Switch-Lit does not claim ownership of your work. By using our platform, you grant us a non-exclusive license to display and promote your story within the Switch-Lit community.

Literary editors can use Switch-Lit to discover fresh collaborative voices and unique narrative perspectives. You can also partner with us to host writing challenges or feature Switch-Lit stories in your publications. Contact us to explore collaboration opportunities.

Cultural programmers can integrate Switch-Lit into festivals, workshops, and community events. We offer group accounts and custom prompts for events. Our platform has been featured at SXSW and Harvard University workshops. Reach out to discuss how we can support your programming.
