Frequently Asked Questions

Switch-Lit is a creative writing platform designed for turn-based collaborative fiction, poetry, and prose. Each work becomes an imaginative conversation between any two creative writers: one starts, the other responds in kind, and the narrative world comes alive via alternating chapters.

Switch-Lit is for anyone who loves storytelling and wants to explore the creative possibilities of collaborative writing. Whether you're an experienced writer or just starting out, if you're curious about co-creating narratives with another person, this platform is for you.

To start a story, click on "Start a Story" and fill out the story settings form. You can invite a writing partner by email, set word and time limits, and decide who writes first. Once your partner accepts, you can begin your collaborative journey!
